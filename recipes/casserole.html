John Wayne Casserole

Description

I made this John Wayne casserole on a lark and it ended up being one of the best one-pan meals I've discovered!

Ingredients

  • Biscuits
  • Beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Vegetables
  • Sour cream and mayonnaise
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the crust
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Cook the veggles
  4. Make the creamy layer
  5. Layer and bake the casserole
